Supporting the Bride

Kira Marjanovic

Maid of Honor

Tess’s Younger Sister. From Europe Trips to early morning Marco’s Pizza Runs, Kira and Tess always share fun adventures when they are together. Kira helped Drew plan one of the biggest adventures to date– his surprise proposal to Tess! She has been by Tess’s side through the good and the bad, and she is so excited to be taking on the Maid of Honor duties and standing by her side on her big day! A crayon-eating Marine, Kira’s mission is to ensure this is the best run wedding to date. SCREAM MARINE CORPS!

Gina Marjanovic

Bridesmaid

Tess’s Youngest Sister. The Tess and Gina Duo are Master Christmas Cookie Bakers, HGTV level demolition and woodworking Pros, and are always up for a fun challenge. Gina is not only a bridesmaid, but also a fashionista and makeup artist for the wedding. Tess is so happy that Gina is following in her footsteps and doing college marching band! Too bad Oregon State has a good football team… Talons Up! Go BEAVS!

Kate McHugh

Bridesmaid

One of Tess’s Best Friends since 7th grade. From sitting next to each other on the bus to every away game and to Disney World during their four years of marching band, to (accidentally) being amazing partners through four years of Model U.N., these two are true partners in crime! Kate and Tess have a lucky friendship where they make time to reconnect whenever they are on the same side of the country, and it’s like nothing has changed! A crazy turn of events made it so that two of Tess’s bridesmaids are going to the same school right now 2,500 miles away– GO BEAVS!

Stephanie DePauw

Bridesmaid

One of Tess’s Best Friends through college at BGSU and beyond! It was at Steph’s apartment that Tess planned her first date with Drew– and that she tried to convince Tess that Drew did, indeed, like her back! They really bonded through leadership in the Falcon Marching Band, their performance related injuries, and sitting next to each other during Kappa Kappa Psi meetings. Steph always has the best advice and is a great listener to all of Tess’s teacher tales.
